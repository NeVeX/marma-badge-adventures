=============================
Fruit Pack - Low Poly Series.
=============================

This package contains 15 ready-to-use low poly models of 11 kinds of fruit.

List of Assets

- Apple (93 tris)
- Banana (164 tris)
- Cherry (129 tris)
- Grape Fruit (95 tris)
- Grape Fruit Cut (145 tris)
- Kiwi (108 tris)
- Kiwi Cut (452 tris)
- Lemon (108 tris)
- Lemon Cut (292 tris)
- Orange (100 tris)
- Orange Cut (284 tris)
- Peach (123 tris)
- Pear (133 tris)
- Strawberry (120 tris)
- Watermelon (464 tris)

Optimized for

- PC
- Mobile
- VR

All models are in .fbx format.
All models use Only one material (1024 x 1024 texture in .png format).
All objects are optimized to have as few triangles as possible without losing quality and style.
